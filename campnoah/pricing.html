So... what does camp cost?

As a non-profit, we aren't in this for the money! Our misson is to provide a fun & safe summer camp at an affordable price. Prices are fair and we discount the cost when you send multiple campers.

$225

First camper

$215

Second camper

$175

Additional campers

What is included?

The camper fees cover lodging Sunday-Friday night, use of the campsite's facilities and paid-staff, three meals per day (and snacks!), and supplies used throughout the week. The Camp NOAH staff are all volunteers and donate their time to run camp.
