A week designed to limit distractions & focus on God.

Our goal at camp, is to limit worldy distractions, focus on God, and study the Bible together. Our campers tell us that reducing wordly influences is one of greatest benefits of attending camp.

While at camp, campers stay busy attending bible classes, athletic events, activity classes, special programs, and free-time with their peers.

Our campers travel from all over.

For the past 20 years we have met in Toledo, Ohio, however our campers come from all over the country! This gives campers the unique opportuntiy to build friendships with their peers outside their normal social circles. Many of these friendships continue on after camp ends and many continue for life.

Experienced and friendly staff.

We've been around 25 years thanks to the dedication and support of our amazing staff! Our leadership, counselors, and staff are an amazing group of men and women who volunteer their time in order to provide a safe, fun, and enriching environment for our campers.
